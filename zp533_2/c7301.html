With the subtle decoration on the razor strong full hollow carbon steel blade and the silky ebony handle, it offers a luxurious shaving experience. Each of these knives is largely handcrafted and leaves Solingen as a unique piece.MaterialThis razor is made of carbon steel with the material designation 1.2210, better known as silver steel. To achieve a hardness of circa 61 HRC, the material is heated to over 1000 degrees and then immediately quenched with a special oil. The subsequent tempering at about 200 degrees gives the blade elasticity and toughness. The ornaments are created by coating with 24 karat gold. Handle materialEbony is one of the most valuable woods on earth. It is extremely hard and heavy. Therefore, it is excellently suited for the manufacture of a high-quality handle. Its structure allows the wood to hold its shape and not swell, even in humid environments. Several grinding passes are necessary to create the silky soft surface. SharpeningWith hollow grinding, the razor is ground out concave from the back to the cutting edge. This design combines great flexibility with high longitudinal torsional rigidity. The blade dynamics ensures a smooth shave accompanied by a very characteristic sound, which is triggered by the swinging of the razor at the hair section. Blade descriptionThe round head is the classic shape of a razor and will meet most shaving situations. This blade design has been popular for centuries, as the bulbous shape is very safe to use around the ears, nose and dimples. Also, in the event of a fall, the round head protects you from injury. Blade widthShaving razors in the 5/8 inch size are real all-rounders and can pass any task around the classic wet shave with flying colors. The blade is relatively heavy and can therefore glide smoothly over your skin without much pressure.
