Bring the benefits of professional non-stick knives, right to the dining table, with this stunning 6-piece non-stick steak knife set Beautifully balanced for unrivaled comfort, each steak knife fits masterfully into the palm of your hand for easy, precise cutting. Jean-Patrique started when a French chef living in the UK made a small order of restaurant quality kitchenware for his family and some friends.
