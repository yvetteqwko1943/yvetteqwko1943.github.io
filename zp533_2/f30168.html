Fleetwood.. -Bonus Tr-

8-CD boxed set that includes remastered versions of all seven studio albums the band recorded between 1969 and 1974 several bonus tracks and an unreleased 1974 concert recorded just a few months before Lindsey Buckingham and Stevie Nicks joined the band.
