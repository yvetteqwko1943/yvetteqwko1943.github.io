Warm Tips:

1. Our knife blade is pre-oiled, it"s not the used product.

2. Avoid cutting frozen food, large bones, l or opening tins.

3. Hand wash it with clean water, and dry the surface with a clean towel after each use.

4. Please apply oil regularly to prevent rust. If it rusts a bit, wipe it with vinegar or toothpaste.

5. Sharpen the knife by hand with a grindstone when it needs sharpening.

6. Keep it in a dry, clean and ventilated place away from children, corrosive liquid, direct sunlight and open fires.
