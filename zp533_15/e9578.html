INSTALLATION MANUAL
1. Skid plate is manufactured from 5 mm thick sheet Aerospace fuselage parts material， to give it Strength， Durability and Light Weighting in Dirty/Off-road conditions and at the same time protecting the Engine/Crank case from Stone/Pellet hits and during event of a fall.
2. The Gussets are welded with parity to the parent material and Powder coated to give the Protection from Corrosion and Color parity (Black) with the Motorbike
3. Fitment of the Skid Plate to the Under chassis uses Specially designed Brackets made out of Stainless steel and compatible Stainless-steel hardware
4. The Skid plate need not be removed during Oil servicing， as there is a provision of a cut-out hole at the bottom， giving it access to Oil drain plug
5. Quick， easy and Do it Yourself fitment and Mounting Instructions can be accessed and downloaded from SRC Web site
6. Skid plate is for Custom exhaust
7. Fits for YAMAHA WR155 R
