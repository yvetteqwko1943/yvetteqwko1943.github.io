EPYC 7551P is a 64-bit 32-core x86AMD EPYC 7551P 32C 2GHz 3GHz 64MB SP3 1P DDR4-2666 180W enterprise server microprocessor introduced by AMD in mid-2017. This processor is based on the Zen microarchitecture and is manufactured on a 14 nm process. The 7551P has a base frequency of 2 GHz with a turbo frequency of 3 GHz for up to 12 active cores. This chip has a TDP of 180 W and supports up to 2 TiB of octa-channel DDR4-2666 ECC memory.
