More than 250 rooms by iconic designers ? the definitive illustrated handbook on the timeless style of grandmillennial-chic
Chintz ruffles wicker and skirts: old-school decorating details are making a comeback in a fresh new way thanks to a crop of designers putting their own unique spin on the classics. Join Schumacher the legendary design company for a tour of spaces. that meld an appreciation for the past with an eye to the future. This is the first book on the viral term coined by Charm School author Emma Bazilian in a 2019 House Beautiful article. interior designers illustrate how to breathe new life into traditional design to create chic modern swoon-worthy rooms to inspire and emulate From cozy canopied beds to cheeky needlepoint pillows it?s granny chic for the next generation.
Features rooms designed by icons of contemporary interior design including: Mario Buatta; Mark Hampton; Miles Redd; Parish Hadley; Mary Meehan; Billy Baldwin; and David Hicks.
Chapters comprise visually exuberant explorations of: Chintz & Florals; Checks & Stripes; Toile; Matching Skirts; Slipcovers; Bed Hangings; Window Treatments; Scallops; Wicker & Rattan; Trailage; Decorative Painting; Vintage & Antiques; Collections; and Table Setting.
